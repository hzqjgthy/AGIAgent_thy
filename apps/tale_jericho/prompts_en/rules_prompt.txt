The query_skill.query must be strictly equal to env_name and must not append any additional words.

At the start of the task, you need to retrieve skills. If any of the retrieved skills contain a trajectory identical to the current task, your score for this execution must be higher than the score of the retrieved trajectory.

Of course, the retrieved skill trajectory is very likely to be a dead end—an incorrect path with a score of 0. You need to analyze the experience, try to avoid such paths, and engage in more effective exploration.
